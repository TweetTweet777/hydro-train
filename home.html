What are hydrogen powered trains?

Welcome to my Website!

Hydrogen powered trains are vehicles which use on-board hydrogen as a source of energy. This Energy powers the traction motors, or the auxiliaries, or both. Hydrogen powered vehicles convert the chemical energy of hydrogen to mechanical energy. This conversion happens either by burning hydrogen in a hydrogen internal combustion engine vehicle, or by reacting hydrogen with oxygen in a fuel cell to run electric motors.
